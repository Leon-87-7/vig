# Ownix

**Your internet, indexed.**

Ownix is a quiet, premium tool for collecting the internet you care about:
videos, links, articles, repos, documents, and ideas. Everything you save
becomes part of your personal Index, organized into a Feed you own and can
return to whenever you need it.

The Brain is the shared layer. Keep your Index private, or choose what
contributes signal to the public Brain, a collective map shaped by the people
using Ownix.

Ownix is invite-only while it is young. Not for exclusivity, but for
conversation: every version bump should be shaped with real users, real
workflows, and honest feedback.
